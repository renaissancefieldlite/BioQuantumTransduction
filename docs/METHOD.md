# Method

This repo is the alignment-testing lane for biosignal-style coupling.

It asks:

- does the alignment metric respond in the expected direction when the model is
  aligned vs misaligned?
- does that behavior persist when the target trace is replaced by a
  calibration-anchored coherence proxy?

The next empirical step is to attach real EEG/HRV or other physiological captures and score them against a non-imposed device trace.

# Method

The repo does not claim that a synthetic alignment result is itself biological
evidence.

It asks a narrower question:

- does the alignment metric respond in the expected direction when the model is
  aligned vs misaligned?
- does that behavior persist when the target trace is replaced by a
  calibration-anchored coherence proxy?

The empirical step is still pending: attach real EEG/HRV or other physiological
captures and score them against a non-imposed device trace.
